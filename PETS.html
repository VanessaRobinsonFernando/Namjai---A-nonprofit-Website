This is a platform where we let you adopt pets that were earlier stray animals. Nam-jai's pet friendly workers give shelter to domestic animals found on the streets especially those who are injured or starving. A small shelter home is provided to the animals and they are given good nutrition and medical attention as well. They are regularly cleaned and well groomed. Our main motive is to provide them with proper homes to get the love and care they require, we indulge in keeping them in a safe environment and pass them on to their interested owner.

All you need to know about the adoption process

  • All the pets are primarly vaccinated ensuring no spread of diseases.
  • Once you adopt a pet, you will get a certificate card that specifies the breed, approximate age, date found and date adopted.
  • We will further provide with a brochure explaining a typical routine for the selected pet, food options and cleanliness measures to be taken.
  • Every once a year we will have a checking process ensuring you are handling your pet with utmost care and for that you will be required to sign a 'No Objection Certificate'(NOC).
